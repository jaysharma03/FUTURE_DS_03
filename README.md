# 📊 Marketing Funnel & Conversion Performance Analysis

## 📌 Project Overview
This project provides a data-driven analysis of marketing campaigns by tracking the full funnel from impressions to revenue. It helps identify high-performing channels, optimize spend, and improve conversion efficiency.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- Analyze end-to-end marketing funnel  
- Measure CTR, CPC, ROI  
- Compare channels, regions, and products  
- Identify trends and optimization opportunities  

---

## 📂 Dataset
**Source:** [Marketing_Analysis](https://github.com/jaysharma03/FUTURE_DS_03/raw/main/Marketing_Funnel_Analysis_%20Task3.pbix)  

🔑**Key Attributes**: Region, Product, Marketing Channel, Quarter, Spend, Clicks , Conversions, Revenue, Impressions  

---

## 🧹 Data Cleaning
- Removed null values & duplicates  
- Standardized column formats  
- Converted numeric fields  
- Ensured data consistency  

---

## 🔍 Key Insights

• Search & Social channels drive higher conversions

• Email marketing is most cost-efficient (low CPC)

• Strong CTR (~13.86%) indicates good engagement

• Drop observed from clicks → conversions

• West & North regions generate higher revenue

• Product B & C contribute most revenue

• Seasonal trends observed across quarters
